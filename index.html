Scratchpad

Scratchpad is a PWA to store notes. Notes can be written in Markdown.

Notes will be stored on your device, in browser’s local storage and on server.

That application is free and open source. It’s not a commercial product, only a « scratch your own itch » app. Code is available on github.

If you’re concerned about privacy and security, I encourage you to host the app yourself by cloning the project on a web server.

More about my projects and contact informations on paulgreg.me.
